Are you expecting a baby?

We are seeking expectant mothers to participate in this research study. We are looking for black women who are not married and are expecting their first child. We need your input to help create a prenatal coparenting program. You may be eligible to receive a gift card for participating!


Do you work with expecting families?

We are seeking professionals who work with expecting families to participate in this reseach study. If you are a: doula, midwife, lactation counselor, doctor, therapist or other professional who regularly sees clients who are expecting, we are looking for your input.
