About The Organization

Great Black Moms is a community-based organization designed to understand the barriers that define black motherhood and work to eliminate them. Our mission is to Great. We define Great as Growing Resilience through Education, Advocacy and Togetherness. Together we strive to build strong communities.

Proposed Research Study

Location: Grand Rapids, Detroit, Kalamazoo Michigan Population: Unmarried Black women expecting their first child and prenatal/postpartum professionals who provide care for a similar demographic.

This study is designed to understand if and how a prenatal coparenting program can help unmarried Black mothers in these select cities. We are now seeking Unmarried Black Moms-to-be to participate in the research. If selected to participate, you will receive a small gift card as a thank you and be entered into a drawing to receive a $100 gift card.

We are also seeking professionals who work with Black expectant moms-to-be in Grand Rapids, Metro Detroit and Kalamazoo Michigan. Anyone who works in the following or similar positions [doctors, nurses, midwives, doulas, lactation consultants, childbirth educators, therapists, WIC professionals, community health workers, social workers, counselors, etc.] is invited to take a brief survey to help inform education offerings for Black Moms.

For more information about the study, please email jordan@greatblackmoms.org
